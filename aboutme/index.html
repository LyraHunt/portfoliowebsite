I'm Lyra Hunt, and I have been drawing since I can remember. I love to create textures and worlds with intricate pen and ink work. I also have a love for computer programming and video game design, which I combine with my pen and ink work to create interactive pieces of digital art. I am most inspired by things that blur the line between the natural and the artificial, such as the Fibonacci sequence in a sunflower or finding hexagons in a honeycomb.
