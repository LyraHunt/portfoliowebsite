A Windy Day

(2023)

An arcade platformer game where you (hopefully) don't get squished by bricks.

Made with Javascript and HTML Canvas.

Play in Browser

Inspired by this article on the physics system used in the platformer Celeste.

This is a video game where you control a guy called Frank while he tries to avoid getting squished by falling bricks. I had the idea for a small game where you have to dodge around falling obstacles and then climb back onto them to reach higher. I found the article above that was written by the producer of Celeste herself that goes over that game's physics system, so I did my best to rewrite it in Javascript. This game is playable on a keyboard with up to 3 players on any modern browser!
